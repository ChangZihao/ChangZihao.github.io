Bio

I am now a third-year Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. Ninghui Sun and Prof. Sa Wang at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ICT-CAS). Prior to my doctoral study, I worked at Huawei's Poincare lab from July 2020 to January 2022. I got my M.S. degree in Computer Architecture from ICT-CAS in 2020, advised by Prof. Weisong Shi and Prof. Yungang Bao. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from Hefei University of Technology in 2017.
My research mainly focuses on datacenter resource management, operating system, and SmartNIC.
